The MCDMT distinguishes paper types based on initial degree of polymerization (DP0) and pH. These parameters in combination with indoor thermal conditions (temperature and relative humidity) calculate the chemical degradation of paper over time. An expected lifetime of a paper can also be calculated when DP0 is expected to be reduced to a critical value for degree of polymerization (DPcrit) which is around 150 to 300.
